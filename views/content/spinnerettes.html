Hindustan Platinum commenced production of precious metal spinnerettes in 1973 and has been serving the man-made fibre industry for nearly a quarter of a century. Infact, the company is the pioneer in spinnerette manufacturing in India and remains in the country’s exclusive maker of spinnerettes to this day. In addition to manufacturing, Hindustan Platinum repairs & refabricates old and worn out spinnerettes, regardless of the original maker.

Hindustan Platinum manufacturer high precision spinnerettes such as :

Precious Metal Spinnerettes for viscose staple fiber; filament, rayon and tyrecords yarn. Clsuter holder fitted with precious metal spinnerettes for viscose staple; Stainless Steel Cup Form & Ring Spinnerettes for acrylic fiber; Tantalum filter used for viscose staple and rayon filament;

Hindustan Platinum spinnerettes are characterised by high uniformity and offer hole diameter ranging from 40 µ and above with ± 1µ tolerance and hole L/D ratio upto 1:2 with capillery length tolerance ± 10µ.
