Hindustan Platinum manufactures Silver & Silver alloyed wires. We export wires to many companies worldwide. Wires are used in fully automatic assembly.

alloys

Pure Silver (Pure Ag)

AgNi is manufactured by powder metallurgy (Sintered). Its salient feature includes low mechanical wear due to higher hardness, low contact resistance almost constant throughout the life of contact, low material transfer, low burning, good arc migration and better arc extinguishing properties. It also has better anti welding property. It has good formability with Nickel upto 20 %. If this percent goes higher, formability reduces a bit, but wear resistance increases. Available in the form of wires, rivets, profiles and contact tips, Silver Nickel contacts can be welded directly.

The company supplies a vast selection of filler metals as each metal joining application requires specific brazing alloy. Brazing material is available in various compositions. The brazing material range includes Silfos brazing alloy consisting of Silver Copper and Phosphorus in various percentages by weight. We also supply ROHS compliant brazing material. Brazing alloys are offered in the form of foils, rods, strips and more.
