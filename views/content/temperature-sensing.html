Platinum and its alloys have long been recognised as reliable sensing elements in high temperature measurement. Hindustan Platinum provides temperature sensing products that gives better process control, consistent product and improved energy conservation. We manufacture thermocouple wires, resistance thermometers, throw-away tips and alfa wires for temperature sensing. We also supply precious metal sheaths, wherever necessary. For optimum accuracy and stability of temperature measuring in the range between 1000°C and 1800°C. Platinum and Platinum/Rhodium thermocouples wires are widely used. Thermocouples wires are produced from high purity metals to maintain accurate e.m.f. values. The accuracy between the relation of temperature and e.m.f. is within the tolerance of international standards.
