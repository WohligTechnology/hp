Hindustan Platinum is the pioneer in manufacturing of electrical contacts in India, with customer base spread across six continents. We manufacture electrical contacts for various applications such as switchgear & control gear, automotive, railways, elevators, telecom, aviation, medical, defence etc. We ensure stringent quality parameters at every stage of manufacturing process to deliver quality products to our customers. This has changed the perception about India from being a part of LCR (Low cost region) to HQR (High quality region).

Hindustan Platinum has a committed team of professionals, who understand customer needs. We have a dedicated team for research & development to create value for our customers. We have been continuously investing in equipments with latest technologies from across the globe to meet ever increasing customer demand, while simultaneously deploying Lean Manufacturing Concepts in Contacts division. The entire manufacturing process is conducted in-house....from refining of precious metal to final manufacturing of products, tailor made as per customer requirement. We provide RoHS & REACH assistance to our customers. We are green channel suppliers to many of our esteemed customers. We manufacture various alloys which are available in different forms.
